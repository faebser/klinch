«Wellness Through Hypnosis #3»

Get the bundle for 150$

MATHIAS FORBACH & NICOLA CASSETTI

SONG: 5.52 MIN. [MP3 320 KBPS]

POSTER: ACRYL ON PAPER – UNIQUE [594×841mm]

KLICH had the possibility to to make an audiovisual session at the Art Book Fair
«i never read» which is a part of the Art Basel. During a nearly two-hour slot,
curated by Vandalizm Records, Marco Nicolas Heinzen aka Nicolas Casetti experimented
with beats and voices. Mathias Forbach [fichtre] created with acrylic on paper,
interpretations to this sound and produced a serie of posters, which is available at KLINCH.

«The challenge at the performance was, to take the listener on the nerves and dissolve
the voltage not too early. A certain intolerance of sound environment had to be unconditional
and could only be loosened with gentle synthesizer interventions for the listener.»

«In a Metamodernen world, everything is simultaneously:
Digital Music and LP's – Lomo photography and digital cameras – industrial
inkjet prints and classic oil painting – war and peace.»

«I think every medium is well when it serves my work as an artist.
Appropriate it – Use it – Violeta it – make it your own»
[Marco Nicolas Heinzen]

«Strukulturen»

Get the bundle for 60$

MARC ZENHÄUSERN & MELODIESINFONIE

SONG: 2.45 MIN. [MP3 320 KBPS]

POSTER: OFFSET [594×841mm]

Producer Kevin Wettstein aka Melodiesinfonie was the first artist who created a song for KLINCH. His song «Strukulturen» consists of numerous fragments such as synthesizers, ethno sounds and soul samples, dancing on a groovy hip-hop beat. The poster was created by the graphic designer Marc Zenhäusern alias mood-wood, who immersed himself into the song and his bits to create a playful, simple black and white poster.

«Glass»

Get the bundle for 50$

REBEKKA SCHAERER & ANDRÉ SEILER

SONG: 4.44 MIN. [MP3 320 KBPS]

POSTER: DIGITALPRINT [594×841mm]

GLASS is the first KLINCH release and was produced by Rebekka Schaerer and André Seiler.
In the atelier of the artist Bernd Kniel, they played with glass. During this session,
the artists took pictures, made films, painted, and collected sounds.

Glass is not simply a fragile crystalline pane It’s molecular structure is referred to as a
«solid liquid». The various options for interpretation made the artists search for and create
flowing forms and sounds but also beats and images which include structure and rhythm.

In a collective work we tried to capture the complexity of glass and reflect
it in pictures and sounds.

«Rust»

Get the bundle for 50$

CLAUDIO GASSER & CLINKER

SONG: 8.31 MIN. [MP3 320 KBPS]

POSTER: SILKSCREEN [594×841mm]

Between industrial buildings, old containers and run-down shacks is Friederike Leuenberger’s
screen printing studio «Durchdruck». Her studio is the location for the KLINCH release
«RUST» with musician Yannick Frich aka Clincker and the graphic designer Claudio Gasser
from the ATLAS studio.

The seedy, industrial atmosphere provides the theme for both song and poster.
The artists transferred patina and defects into their pieces and created a complex work.
In a collective work we tried to capture the complexity of glass and reflect it
in pictures and sounds.


«Since there is no time for searching solutions you work much more intuitively.
I think the result is simple since the constraints of screen printing let you work in a
much less complicated way.

In the format «poster and song», I see the chance of the haptic dimension bringing about
this «want to have»-wish in combination with the music.
I think this kind of release is for enthusiasts and not for a broader audience.»
[Claudio Gasser]
